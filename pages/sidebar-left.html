Accommodation and Location

Only a 2-minute walk Cheonho Rodeo Street, Plaza Motel offers air-conditioned rooms within a 5-minute walk from Cheonho Subway Station (lines 5 and 8). Featuring a wooden floor, rooms have a computer, a fridge and bathrobes. Some come with a bathtub. Rooms are furnished with a double bed, a seating area and a desk. The bathroom provides with a shower, a toilet and a selection of toiletries..

Greece is conveniently located 162 metres from Gil-Dong subway station (Line no. 5) and a 10-minute walk from a range of dining options. It features rooms with a bathtub and a personal computer with free internet access.
The hotel is situated next to the Incheon airport limousine bus station and is just a 5-minute drive from Chenho Station (Line no. 5, 8).
